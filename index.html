about us

Comprehensive Cybersecurity & Data Solutions

DCE Infosec is a minority-owned business specializing in comprehensive cybersecurity, data engineering, and staffing and recruitment services. Our mission is to provide top-tier solutions that protect, manage, and optimize your business operations. We leverage cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices to deliver customized solutions that meet our clients' unique needs.

Our Core Services

We offer comprehensive solutions in three key areas to help protect and optimize your business.

Cyber Security

Comprehensive security solutions including Incident Response, SIEM, SOAR, IAM, Endpoint Security, and more. We utilize industry-leading tools and best practices to protect your digital assets.

Data Engineering

Expert data services including cleaning, preprocessing, exploration, visualization, and governance. We help you transform raw data into actionable insights using cutting-edge tools and methodologies.

Staffing Solutions

Comprehensive recruitment services including talent identification, screening, assessment, and onboarding support. We help you find the right professionals for your organization.

OpenSearch SIEM

Next-Gen SIEM powered by OpenSearch - advanced log management, real-time analytics, and threat detection with our fully managed solution. Learn more

Security Operations Center (SOC)

24/7 security monitoring and incident response services. Our SOC team provides continuous threat detection, analysis, and response to protect your organization from cyber threats.

Government Services

Specialized cybersecurity and data solutions for government agencies. We provide secure, compliant, and efficient services tailored to meet federal, state, and local government needs. Learn more

ELK Stack SIEM

Enterprise-grade SIEM solution powered by the Elastic Stack with official support and features. Advanced log management, real-time analytics, and threat detection capabilities. Learn more

Detection & SIEM Engineering

Custom detection rule development, SIEM platform deployment, and optimization services. We help build effective, scalable detection capabilities across modern SIEM platforms. Learn more
