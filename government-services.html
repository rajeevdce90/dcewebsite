Company Overview

DCE Infosec LLC is a minority-owned business dedicated to delivering exceptional cybersecurity, data engineering, and staffing and recruitment services. We aim to safeguard, manage, and optimize business operations through cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices. Our tailored solutions are designed to meet the unique needs of our clients, ensuring robust security and operational efficiency.

Core Capabilities

  • Incident Response: Rapid incident containment and forensic analysis.
  • SIEM: Centralized security event data and anomaly detection.
  • SOAR: Automated security operations and incident management.
  • Alert Noise Reduction: Focus on critical threats.
  • IAM: Secure access control and multi-factor authentication.
  • Endpoint Security: Device protection with anti-virus and encryption.
  • Vulnerability Management: Continuous assessment and patch management.
  • Network Security: Network protection and firewall management.
  • DLP: Prevention of unauthorized data access.
  • Threat Detection: Real-time threat identification and analysis.
  • Network Device Deployment: Configuration of routers and firewalls.
  • WAP Deployment: Secure wireless network setup.
  • Data Engineering: Data processing and security.
  • Staffing Services: Comprehensive recruitment and compliance support.

Key Differentiators

  • Tailored Solutions: Customization of services to meet specific client needs.
  • Cutting-Edge Technologies: Utilization of the latest tools and best practices.
  • Comprehensive Security: End-to-end cybersecurity solutions.
  • Diverse Expertise: Skilled professionals across multiple domains.
  • Client-Centric Approach: Strong focus on client satisfaction and relationship management.
